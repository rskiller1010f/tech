Last updated: January 2024

Important: TechLearnHub is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.in.

What This Means

As an Amazon Associate, TechLearnHub earns from qualifying purchases. This means that when you click on product links on our website and make a purchase on Amazon, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you.

How Affiliate Links Work

When you click on a product link on TechLearnHub:

  • You are redirected to Amazon.in (or the relevant Amazon marketplace)
  • Amazon tracks that you came from our website using a special affiliate link
  • If you make a purchase within a certain time period (typically 24 hours), we earn a small percentage of the sale
  • The price you pay remains exactly the same - there is no extra charge to you

Our Commitment to Honesty

We want to be completely transparent about our affiliate relationships:

  • No Extra Cost: You never pay more for products purchased through our links. The commission comes from Amazon, not from your pocket.

  • Editorial Independence: Our affiliate relationship with Amazon does not influence our content or the products we choose to feature. We maintain complete editorial independence.
  • Honest Reviews: We strive to provide honest, balanced information about products, including any potential drawbacks or limitations.

Why We Use Affiliate Links

Running a website requires time, effort, and resources. The affiliate commissions we earn help us:

  • Keep the website running and maintained

Your support through these affiliate links enables us to continue helping Indian consumers find great budget tech products.
